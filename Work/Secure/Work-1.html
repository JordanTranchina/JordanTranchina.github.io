AMEXplore | Imagining the future of travel
Situation
AMEX wanted to identify how to play a role in the future of travel so they sponsored a round of Idealab, a 12 week-long Design Thinking case competition. I had just completed my first consulting engagement and was itching to expand on my design skills as I took a role in the Innovation Center as their chief designer.
Task
Leverage Design Thinking to imagine the future of travel and how AMEX plays a part in it.
Action
I worked with a team of consultants to build a prototype of AMEXplore, an app that uses machine learning to provide personalized itineraries with activities sourced from similar users, local card members, and the user’s spending history.
Result
